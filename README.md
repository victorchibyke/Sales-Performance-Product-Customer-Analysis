# 📊 RetailNova Revenue, Product & Customer Intelligence Analysis
Sales Performance, Product Analysis and Customer Intelligence Dashboard built with Excel

# Business Introduction
RetailNova Ltd. is a mid-sized FMCG company operating across multiple regions, offering products including Bakery, Beverages, Fresh Produce, Home Cleaning, Personal Care, Snacks, and Stationery. The company has accumulated over 52,000 transactions from nearly 10,000 customers between 2019 and 2024.

As the business expanded, management required a centralized dashboard to monitor revenue, profitability, customer purchasing behavior, and product performance in order to support strategic business decisions.

# Problem Statement

RetailNova's management lacked a centralized reporting system capable of providing actionable insights into overall business performance. Business leaders needed answers to key questions such as:

Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
Which products are underperforming?
Who are the company's highest-value customers?
How does revenue vary across customer age groups?
Which locations contribute the most customers?
How are monthly sales and profits trending over time?
Which business areas require improvement?

The objective of this project was to transform raw transactional data into an interactive Excel dashboard that provides meaningful business insights.

# Project Objectives

The project aims to:

Analyze overall revenue and profitability.
Evaluate product category performance.
Identify top-performing and underperforming products.
Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
Measure customer distribution across locations.
Monitor monthly sales and profit trends.
Provide actionable business recommendations.

# Monthly Sales Performance

Sales followed an upward trend throughout the year.

Observations
Sales increased steadily from January.
Minor declines occurred during August and September.
Strong recovery began in October.
December recorded the highest monthly revenue.
Business Insight

Seasonal demand significantly influences sales performance, particularly during the fourth quarter.

# Monthly Profit Trend

Profit closely mirrors sales performance.

Observations
Stable profit growth throughout the year.
Temporary slowdown during August–September.
Highest profitability achieved during October–December.
Business Recommendation

Increase inventory and marketing investment before Q4 to maximize revenue opportunities.
